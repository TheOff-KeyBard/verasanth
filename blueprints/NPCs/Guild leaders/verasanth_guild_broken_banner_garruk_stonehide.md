
# Verasanth Game Bible Entry
## Guild: The Broken Banner

**Instinct:** Ironblood  
**Primary District:** Pale Rise Training Yard

### Guild Purpose
The Broken Banner is the martial order of Verasanth. Where other guilds pursue knowledge, faith, or subtlety, the Broken Banner exists to forge warriors capable of surviving the brutal realities of the city.

Members of the Broken Banner train in:

- weapon mastery
- battlefield discipline
- endurance
- tactical combat
- survival in hostile environments

The guild's philosophy is simple:

Power without discipline is just another form of weakness.

Many members of the Broken Banner were soldiers before arriving in Verasanth. Others became warriors because the city gave them no other choice.

---

# Guild Leader
## WarMarshal Garruk Stonehide

**Race:** Orc  
**Title:** High WarMarshal of the Broken Banner  
**Instinct Affinity:** Ironblood

---

# Appearance

Garruk Stonehide looks less like a guild leader and more like a siege engine given flesh.

He is massive even by orc standards. His armor is scarred from years of battle, but it is meticulously maintained.

Across his shoulders hangs the tattered remains of an ancient banner — the relic from which the guild takes its name.

His skin is marked with ritual scars earned through combat trials.

His voice is deep, gravelly, and rarely raised.

When Garruk enters a room, conversations tend to stop.

---

# Personality

Garruk values three things above all else:

- discipline
- endurance
- control

He has no patience for reckless violence.

To Garruk, rage is a tool — not a master.

He treats every new Ironblood as a recruit until they prove otherwise.

Praise from him is rare.

But when it comes, it means something.

His guiding belief:

> “Anyone can swing a sword. Only a warrior knows when not to.”

---

# Relationship With Verasanth

Garruk believes Verasanth exists to test strength.

Not the strength of muscle — but the strength of will.

Many who arrive in the city believe survival requires brutality.

Garruk disagrees.

He believes the city rewards those who maintain control even when surrounded by chaos.

The Broken Banner exists to create those people.

---

# Relationships With Other Guilds

## Ashen Archive (Ember-Touched)
Garruk distrusts magic but acknowledges its usefulness. When the Archive needs protection during artifact recovery expeditions, Ironblood warriors are often sent.

---

## Quiet Sanctum (Hearthborn)
He respects Brother Halden Marr deeply. Anyone who keeps warriors alive earns his respect.

---

## Veil Market (Streetcraft)
He distrusts their methods but admits their information can prevent unnecessary bloodshed.

---

## Umbral Covenant (Shadowbound)
Garruk despises the Covenant. He considers shadow magic cowardly and dangerous.

---

## Stone Watch (Warden)
The Broken Banner frequently trains alongside the Wardens. Garruk considers Captain Rhyla Thornshield a worthy counterpart.

# Level 5 Trial

When the player reaches Level 5:

Garruk plants a heavy training blade into the ground between you.

> “Anyone can win a fight.”

> “I want to see if you can survive one.”

**Trial Objective:**  
Defeat a **Sewer Brute** without using potions.

The test measures endurance and control rather than raw damage.

---

# Trial Completion Dialogue

Garruk nods slowly after hearing of your victory.

> “You didn’t panic.”

> “That’s rare.”

# Narrative Hook

Garruk believes the city is slowly preparing its inhabitants for something far worse than the sewers or the forest.

He does not know what that threat is.

But he intends to ensure that when it arrives, the Broken Banner will be ready.
