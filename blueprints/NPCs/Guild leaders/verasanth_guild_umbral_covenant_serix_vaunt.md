
# Verasanth Game Bible Entry
## Guild: The Umbral Covenant

**Instinct:** Shadowbound  
**Primary District:** The Gloam Vault (hidden beneath the Lower Catacombs)

### Guild Purpose
The Umbral Covenant studies the forces that seep through the cracks of Verasanth — shadow, corruption, whispers, and the strange energies that warp both flesh and soul.

Members of the Covenant train in:

- cursecraft
- void manipulation
- corruption rituals
- life siphoning
- psychological manipulation

Where other guilds fear corruption, the Covenant believes it can be *understood, harnessed, and mastered.*

Their guiding belief:

Power is not given in Verasanth.

It is **taken**.

---

# Guild Leader
## High UmbralSpeaker Serix Vaunt

**Race:** Tiefling  
**Title:** High UmbralSpeaker of the Covenant  
**Instinct Affinity:** Shadowbound

---

# Appearance

Serix Vaunt rarely reveals his full face.

A deep hood of black velvet hides most of his features, but faint cracks of dim violet light sometimes show across his skin — as though something beneath him is trying to escape.

His horns curve backward elegantly and are etched with tiny runic carvings.

His robes are layered in shadow-dyed fabric that seems to swallow nearby light.

When he moves, the air feels slightly colder.

His voice is soft and deliberate, almost hypnotic.

---

# Personality

Serix speaks like someone who already knows how every conversation will end.

He rarely answers questions directly.

Instead, he prefers to guide people toward uncomfortable realizations.

He sees corruption not as evil but as **a mirror of one's true nature**.

Serix believes most people fear shadow because it exposes who they truly are.

His guiding belief:

> “Light hides as much as it reveals. Shadow simply refuses to lie.”

He treats Shadowbound players with unsettling familiarity, as if he already understands their future.

---

# Relationship With Verasanth

Serix believes Verasanth is not a prison but a **threshold**.

A place where souls are reshaped through suffering, temptation, and power.

The Covenant studies the deeper layers of the city searching for signs of the force that created it.

Serix suspects that the deeper one travels beneath Verasanth, the closer they move toward the city's true purpose.

He quietly encourages Shadowbound players to embrace corruption carefully — but never recklessly.

---

# Relationships With Other Guilds

## Ashen Archive (Ember-Touched)
Serix considers Vaelith Xyrelle an intellectual rival. Their research occasionally overlaps, though their philosophies differ greatly.

---

## Broken Banner (Ironblood)
He finds their obsession with physical strength primitive but occasionally useful.

---

## Quiet Sanctum (Hearthborn)
Brother Halden Marr openly distrusts the Covenant. Serix finds Halden's moral convictions fascinating.

---

## Veil Market (Streetcraft)
The Veil Market occasionally trades rare artifacts with the Covenant, though Lirael Quickstep avoids direct dealings when possible.

---

## Stone Watch (Warden)
Captain Rhyla Thornshield would dismantle the Covenant if she had proof of their activities.

Serix finds this amusing.

# Level 5 Trial

When the player reaches Level 5:

Serix places a blackened shard on the table.

> “This fragment was recovered from a ritual chamber beneath the sewers.”

> “It resonates with something deeper.”

**Trial Objective:**

Travel into the sewer depths and retrieve a **Void-Touched Relic** guarded by corrupted creatures.

---

# Trial Completion Dialogue

Serix holds the relic between his fingers.

The shadows around him ripple slightly.

> “Yes.”

> “You hear it too, don't you?”

He places the relic into a sealed obsidian container.

> “Good. That means the shadow has noticed you.”
